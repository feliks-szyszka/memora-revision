Getting Started

Memora is a powerful flashcard study tool designed to help you learn and retain information efficiently. Here's how to get started:

Create Packs: Organize flashcards by subject and folder
Study Mode: Use spaced repetition to master your cards
Import/Export: Share packs or back up your data
Mobile Ready: Study anywhere on any device

How to Use

1. Creating Flashcard Packs

Click "Create Pack" and choose a subject, optional folder name, and pack name. This helps you organize your study materials by topic.

2. Adding Flashcards

Once you've created a pack, add flashcards by clicking "Add Card". Enter a question on the front and the answer on the back.

3. Studying & Mastering Cards

Click "Study Now" on any pack to enter study mode. Here's how it works:

  • Flip: Click the card or Flip button to see the answer
  • Master: Once you've seen the answer, click "Got It!" to mark the card as mastered
  • Navigate: Use Previous/Next to move between cards
  • Track Progress: Mastered cards count toward your daily goal and streak

4. Building Your Streak

Study every day to build your streak! Each day you master at least one card, your streak increases. Your dashboard shows your current streak, cards mastered today, and total packs.

Using AI to Generate Flashcards

You can use AI tools like ChatGPT or Claude to generate flashcard packs. Here's the JSON format Memora uses:

💡 Pro Tip: Copy the JSON format below and ask an AI to create flashcards for any topic!

JSON Format Structure

{ "Subject Name": { "folders": { "Folder Name": [ { "id": "unique-id-123", "name": "Pack Name", "cards": [ { "question": "What is the capital of France?", "answer": "Paris", "created": "2024-01-01T12:00:00.000Z" } ], "created": "2024-01-01T12:00:00.000Z" } ] } } }

Importing AI-Generated Packs

  1. Ask an AI to create flashcards in the JSON format above
  2. Copy the generated JSON and save it as a .json file
  3. Go to the Library page in Memora
  4. Click "Import JSON" and select your file
  5. Your new flashcard pack will be imported automatically!

Study Tips

  • Build your streak: Study at least one card every day
  • Active recall: Try to answer before flipping the card
  • Honest assessment: Only mark cards as mastered when you truly know the answer
  • Space it out: Review packs multiple times over several days
  • Export backups: Regularly export your packs to save your progress

Data Storage

All your flashcard data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • ✅ Your data stays private and on your device
  • ✅ No internet connection required after loading the page
  • ⚠️ Clearing browser data will delete your flashcards
  • 💾 Use Export/Import to back up your packs
